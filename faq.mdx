# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

<Accordion title="What is Slinq?">
  Slinq is a social token factory for Ethereum and BNB Chain. You can create tradeable tokens directly from Twitter, Farcaster, Telegram, or our web interface. Launches route through:
  - Ethereum via Slinq's Clanker wrapper
  - BNB Chain via PancakeSwap Infinity or V3
  - Creator revenue for the token creator or beneficiary
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How is Slinq different from pump.fun or Four.meme?">
  **Creator rewards:** Slinq routes creator revenue to the token creator or beneficiary. On BNB Chain hook launches, creators can receive up to 40% of the hook fee.

  **No bonding curve:** Slinq tokens launch directly onto their trading rail — Clanker-compatible Ethereum launches or PancakeSwap BNB Chain launches.

  **Social tokenization:** Reply "tokenize this" to any viral post to turn it into a token. The original author earns fees.

  **Multi-platform:** Create tokens from Twitter, Farcaster, Telegram, or web — not just one interface.

  **AI-powered:** Slinq's AI generates names, tickers, and descriptions automatically for social launches.

  **Dual-chain design:** Ethereum for Clanker-native distribution, BNB Chain for PancakeSwap-native launches.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is Slinq safe?">
  Slinq is designed with safety in mind:
  - All liquidity is permanently locked (no rugs)
  - Smart contracts are open source
  - MEV protection prevents bot exploitation
  - No team tokens or hidden allocations

  However, memecoins are inherently risky. Only invest what you can afford to lose.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What blockchain does Slinq use?">
  Slinq supports **Ethereum Mainnet** (Chain ID 1) and **BNB Chain** (Chain ID 56). Ethereum launches use Slinq's Clanker wrapper. BNB Chain launches use PancakeSwap Infinity or V3.
</Accordion>

## Token Creation

<Accordion title="What is social tokenization?">
  Social tokenization lets you turn any viral tweet or Farcaster cast into a tradeable token by replying with a keyword like "tokenize this" (plus the bot mention). The **original post author** automatically becomes the token creator and earns fees. Learn more on our [social tokenization page](/create/social-tokenization).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How much does it cost to create a token?">
  Token creation is **free** (gasless). You only need to sign a message with your wallet — no transaction fees for deployment.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I edit my token after launching?">
  No. Token parameters (name, symbol, supply, image) are permanent and immutable. Double-check everything before launching.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What's the token supply?">
  All Slinq tokens have a fixed supply of **100 billion** tokens. This cannot be changed.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I set a custom supply?">
  No. The 100 billion supply is fixed for all tokens to ensure consistency across the platform.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What's FDV and how do I choose it?">
  FDV (Fully Diluted Valuation) is the total market cap at launch. It determines your token's initial price.

  - **Ethereum:** 1 / 2 / 5 / 10 ETH presets, with 2 ETH as the default
  - **BNB Chain:** 50-50,000 BNB in the web flow, with 10 BNB as the common default
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can someone copy my token?">
  Tickers are unique—no one can use the same symbol. However, anyone can create a similarly named token. Build your community to establish authenticity.
</Accordion>

## Trading

<Accordion title="Where are tokens traded?">
  Ethereum Slinq tokens trade through the Clanker wrapper route. BNB Chain tokens trade on PancakeSwap Infinity or V3 through our integrated interface.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What are the trading fees?">
  Fees depend on the launch rail:
  - **Ethereum:** creator-facing Clanker/Slinq revenue shown in ETH terms
  - **BNB Chain Infinity:** 1% base hook fee, with up to 40% of hook fees for the creator
  - **BNB Chain V3:** creator rewards come from harvested LP fees
  - Plus ETH or BNB gas fees

  If someone else triggered the launch of your token, the split is 35% creator + 5% first buyer instead of 40% creator.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What are hooks?">
  Hooks are PancakeSwap V4's plugin system. Slinq uses a hook that runs automatically on every trade to calculate fees, protect against bots, and distribute earnings to creators. You don't need to do anything — it all happens behind the scenes.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is MEV protection?">
  MEV (Maximal Extractable Value) bots try to exploit new launches by front-running and sandwiching trades. Slinq's hook can raise buy fees as high as 5% during the first 30 seconds, then it settles back to the 1% base fee. Sell fees stay at 1%. This makes sniping much less attractive.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Why did my transaction fail?">
  Common reasons:
  - Insufficient ETH or BNB for gas
  - Price moved too much (slippage)
  - Network congestion

  Try again with more of the correct chain currency in your wallet.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I trade on other DEXs?">
  Yes. Slinq tokens are standard tokens on their launch chain. BNB Chain tokens are BEP-20; Ethereum tokens are ERC-20. Trading outside Slinq may have different routing or fees.
</Accordion>

## Creator Rewards

<Accordion title="How do I earn as a creator?">
  You automatically earn creator revenue on your token. Ethereum launches expose the creator-facing Clanker/Slinq revenue in your dashboard. On BNB Chain hook launches, self-launched tokens receive 40% of the hook fee; if someone else triggered the on-chain launch, the creator receives 35% and the initializer receives 5%.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if someone else tokenized my post?">
  You still earn as the creator! When someone replies "tokenize this" to your tweet or cast, you're automatically set as the token creator. You earn 35% of all trading fees (the person who triggered the launch earns 5%). Fees accumulate even before you connect a wallet — just sign in later to claim.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="When can I claim my fees?">
  Anytime. There's no minimum or waiting period. Just visit your creator dashboard and click "Claim."
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Are fees paid in BNB or the token?">
  BNB Chain hook fees are paid in **WBNB** (Wrapped BNB), which is equivalent to BNB. Ethereum creator revenue follows the ETH claim flow.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if no one trades my token?">
  No trades = no fees. Focus on building community and utility to drive trading activity.
</Accordion>

## Season Pass

<Accordion title="How do I earn points?">
  Three ways:
  - **Trading:** points based on normalized trading volume
  - **Creating:** points based on creator fee revenue
  - **Referring:** 25% of your referrals' points
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do points expire?">
  Points last until the season ends. When a new season starts, points reset to zero.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What do I get for points?">
  Points determine your rank on the leaderboard. Top performers receive rewards at season end. Higher tiers also unlock point multipliers.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I transfer or sell points?">
  No. Points are non-transferable and tied to your wallet.
</Accordion>

## Technical

<Accordion title="What wallet should I use?">
  Any Ethereum and BNB Chain compatible wallet:
  - MetaMask
  - WalletConnect
  - Coinbase Wallet
  - Embedded wallet (via email sign-in)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I add BNB Chain to my wallet?">
  Most wallets add it automatically. If needed:
  - Network Name: BNB Smart Chain
  - RPC: https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org
  - Chain ID: 56
  - Symbol: BNB
  - Explorer: https://bscscan.com
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Where can I view my tokens?">
  - In your wallet (may need to import the token address)
  - On BscScan using your wallet address
  - On the "My Tokens" page when connected to Slinq
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What's the contract address for my token?">
  Find it on your token's Slinq page or in the confirmation tweet/cast when you created it.
</Accordion>
