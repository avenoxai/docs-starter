# How Fees Accumulate

Every trade on your token generates fees. Here's how those fees flow to you.

## The Fee Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Trade Happens">
    A user buys or sells your token on Slinq.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hook Calculates Fee">
    The PancakeSwap V4 hook automatically calculates the fee (1% base + any dynamic premiums).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hook Splits the Fee">
    The fee is distributed: up to 40% to your creator wallet, 60% to the protocol.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Balance Updates">
    Your claimable creator balance increases automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim When Ready">
    You can claim your accumulated fees at any time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Fee Collection Mechanics

### What Generates Fees

Every trade in your token's pool generates fees:

| Action | Fee Generated | Your Share |
|--------|--------------|------------|
| User buys token | 1% base fee, plus any active launch or volatility premium | 0.35-0.4% of the hook fee |
| User sells token | 1% base fee, plus any active volatility premium | 0.35-0.4% of the hook fee |

Your exact share is 40% if you launched the token yourself, or 35% if someone else triggered the on-chain launch (see [fee beneficiaries](/trading/fees#fee-beneficiaries)).

### Fee Accumulation

Your rewards accumulate onchain as claimable creator fees:

```
Your Balance = Sum of (Your Share % × Hook Fee Collected) across all trades
```

<Info>
Fees are paid in WBNB (Wrapped BNB), which is equivalent to BNB and automatically unwrapped when you claim.
</Info>

## Routing Rewards to Third Parties

Creators aren't limited to receiving rewards in their own wallet. You can **route your fee share to any address** — for example:

- **A charity wallet** — donate your trading fee earnings to a cause
- **A GitHub profile** — support an open-source contributor
- **A community treasury** — fund ecosystem development
- **Any wallet address** — split rewards however you like

To change your fee recipient, visit your token's details page and update the fee beneficiary. The change takes effect immediately for all future trades.

<Tip>
This makes Slinq tokens a tool for sustainable funding — creators can launch tokens where trading activity automatically funds the people and causes they care about.
</Tip>

## Common Questions

<Accordion title="How often are fees collected?">
  Fees accumulate with every trade. The balance in the fee locker updates in real-time.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a minimum to claim?">
  There's no minimum. However, claiming costs a small gas fee, so it's more efficient to wait until you've accumulated a meaningful amount.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can fees be lost?">
  No. Fees are tracked onchain and can only be claimed to the configured beneficiary wallet.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if my token has no trading?">
  No trades = no fees. Focus on building community and utility to drive trading activity.
</Accordion>
