# Creator Rewards

When you create a token on Slinq, you earn up to 40% of all trading fees — forever. This is the highest creator share in the industry, powered by PancakeSwap V4 hooks that automatically split fees on every trade.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your token">
    Launch via Twitter, Farcaster, Telegram, social tokenization, or the web interface.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Trading begins">
    Users buy and sell your token on PancakeSwap.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hook splits fees automatically">
    The Slinq hook on every trade calculates fees and sends your share to the fee locker contract.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim anytime">
    Withdraw your accumulated fees whenever you want from your creator dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Why 40%?

Most token platforms give creators **nothing** from trading fees:

| Platform | Creator Share |
|----------|---------------|
| **Slinq** | **40%** |
| Four.meme | 0% |
| Pump.fun | 0% |
| Friend.tech | 5% |

Slinq believes creators should benefit from the communities they build.

## Fee Splits

Your share depends on how the token was launched:

| How the Token Was Created | Your Share | First Buyer | Protocol |
|--------------------------|-----------|-------------|----------|
| You launched it yourself | **40%** | — | 60% |
| Someone else tokenized your post | **30%** | 10% | 60% |

<Info>
When someone replies "tokenize this" to your tweet or cast, you're automatically set as the creator. If a different person triggers the on-chain launch (the "first buyer"), they earn a 10% share as a reward, and you still get 30%.
</Info>
