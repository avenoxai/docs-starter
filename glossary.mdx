# Glossary

Common terms you'll encounter when using Slinq.

## A

**AMM (Automated Market Maker)**
A decentralized exchange mechanism that uses liquidity pools instead of order books. PancakeSwap is an AMM.

## B

**BEP-20**
The token standard on BNB Chain (similar to ERC-20 on Ethereum). BNB Chain Slinq tokens are BEP-20.

**BNB Chain**
One of Slinq's supported networks. Formerly known as Binance Smart Chain (BSC). BNB Chain Slinq launches trade through PancakeSwap.

## C

**Cast**
A post on Farcaster (similar to a tweet). You can create tokens by casting at `@slinq`.

**Concentrated Liquidity**
A liquidity provision method where funds are focused in specific price ranges. PancakeSwap V4 uses this for better capital efficiency.

**Creator**
The account or wallet assigned as the token creator. Creators earn chain-specific creator revenue.

**Clanker Wrapper**
Slinq's Ethereum launch rail. It uses Slinq's wrapper around Clanker's SDK and contracts so Slinq tokens can launch through the Clanker-compatible Ethereum flow.

## D

**DEX (Decentralized Exchange)**
An exchange that operates without a central authority. PancakeSwap is a DEX.

**Ethereum**
One of Slinq's supported networks. Ethereum launches use Slinq's Clanker wrapper and trade in ETH/WETH terms.

## F

**FDV (Fully Diluted Valuation)**
The total market cap if all tokens were in circulation. Calculated as: Token Price × Total Supply.

**Fee Beneficiary**
The wallet that receives a share of trading fees. Usually the token creator (40%), but when someone else triggers the launch, the split becomes 35% creator + 5% first buyer. Creators can route their rewards to any third-party wallet — for example, to a charity, a GitHub contributor, or any address supporting the ecosystem.

**Fee Locker**
The smart contract that holds accumulated creator fees until claimed.

**First Buyer**
The person who executes the on-chain launch transaction for a token. In social tokenization, the first buyer earns a 5% share of trading fees as a reward.

## G

**Gas**
The fee paid to process transactions. Paid in ETH on Ethereum and BNB on BNB Chain.

## H

**Holder**
A wallet that owns tokens. Holder count is displayed on each token page.

**Hook**
A PancakeSwap V4 smart contract plugin that runs automatically on BNB Chain Infinity trades. Slinq's hook handles dynamic fee calculation, MEV protection, and creator fee distribution for that launch rail.

## L

**Liquidity**
The availability of tokens for trading. More liquidity = better prices and lower slippage.

**LP (Liquidity Pool)**
A pool of tokens locked in a smart contract that enables trading. Slinq tokens pair with WETH on Ethereum or WBNB on BNB Chain.

**LP Lock**
When liquidity is permanently deposited and cannot be withdrawn. All Slinq tokens have locked LP.

## M

**Market Cap**
The total value of all tokens in circulation. For Slinq tokens: Token Price × Circulating Supply.

**MEV (Maximal Extractable Value)**
Profit extracted by reordering, inserting, or censoring transactions. Slinq's MEV protection reduces this exploitation.

**Memecoin**
A cryptocurrency created primarily for entertainment or community, often based on memes or internet culture.

## P

**PancakeSwap**
The largest DEX on BNB Chain. BNB Chain Slinq tokens trade on PancakeSwap Infinity or V3.

**Points**
Season Pass points earned through trading, creating, and referrals.

**Price Impact**
How much a trade moves the market price. Larger trades have bigger impact.

## R

**Referral**
When you invite someone to Slinq using your code. Referrers earn 25% of their referees' points.

**Rug Pull**
When a project's creators withdraw liquidity, making the token worthless. Slinq prevents this with locked LP.

## S

**Season**
A competitive period for the Season Pass. Points reset when a new season begins.

**Slippage**
The difference between expected and actual trade prices. Caused by price movement between quote and execution.

**Smart Contract**
Self-executing code on the blockchain. Slinq's contracts handle token creation, trading, and fee distribution.

**Social Feed**
A dedicated page on Slinq showing tokens created through social tokenization (reply-based launches from Twitter and Farcaster).

**Social Tokenization**
The process of turning a social media post into a tradeable token by replying with a keyword like "tokenize this". The original author becomes the creator.

## T

**Ticker/Symbol**
The short identifier for a token (e.g., $MOON, $DEGEN). Limited to 1-10 characters.

**Token**
A digital asset on the blockchain. Slinq creates ERC-20 tokens on Ethereum and BEP-20 tokens on BNB Chain.

## V

**Volume**
The total value of tokens traded over a period. Usually measured in ETH, BNB, or USD.

## W

**Wallet**
Software that stores your private keys and lets you interact with the blockchain. Examples: MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet.

**WBNB (Wrapped BNB)**
An ERC-20 compatible version of BNB. Used in DeFi protocols. 1 WBNB = 1 BNB always.

**WETH (Wrapped ETH)**
An ERC-20 compatible version of ETH. Used in DeFi protocols. 1 WETH = 1 ETH.
