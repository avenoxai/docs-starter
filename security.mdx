# Security

Slinq is designed with security as a priority. Here's how we protect you.

## Smart Contract Security

### Locked Liquidity

Every Slinq token has **permanently locked liquidity**:

- LP tokens are sent to the LP Locker contract
- There is no withdraw function
- Not even the Slinq team can unlock liquidity
- This prevents "rug pulls" where creators steal funds

<Check>
Once liquidity is locked, it cannot be removed—ever. Your trading pool is permanent.
</Check>

### Immutable Token Parameters

After launch, token properties cannot be changed:

| Parameter | Changeable? |
|-----------|-------------|
| Name | No |
| Symbol | No |
| Supply | No |
| Image | No |
| Creator wallet | No |

<Info>
Immutability protects traders from bait-and-switch schemes where creators change token details after launch.
</Info>

### No Team Tokens

Slinq tokens have:
- 100% of supply in the liquidity pool
- 0% team allocation
- 0% pre-mine
- No hidden wallets

The creator only earns through trading fees, not token holdings.

## MEV Protection

### What is MEV?

MEV (Maximal Extractable Value) attacks include:
- **Front-running:** Bots buying before your transaction to profit from price impact
- **Sandwiching:** Bots buying before and selling after your trade
- **Sniping:** Bots grabbing tokens in the first block of a launch

### How We Protect You

Slinq's launch protection uses dynamic buy fees during the first 30 seconds after launch. The total hook fee is capped at 5% and then settles back to the 1% base fee.

| Time After Launch | Buy Fee | Sell Fee |
|-------------------|---------|----------|
| 0 seconds | 5% | 1% |
| 10 seconds | ~3.2% | 1% |
| 20 seconds | ~1.6% | 1% |
| 30 seconds+ | 1% | 1% |

The MEV premium only applies to **buy orders** — sells always pay the standard 1%. This makes sniping and front-running unprofitable while letting regular users trade fairly.

## Fee Distribution Security

### Smart Contract Custody

Creator rewards are tracked onchain and can only be claimed by the wallet currently set to receive them:

- Not controlled by Slinq team
- Only the designated beneficiary wallet can receive claims
- Cannot be redirected or stolen
- Transparent on-chain

### Claim Safety

When you claim fees:
- Only your wallet can initiate the claim
- Funds go directly to your wallet
- No intermediaries or custodians

## Platform Security

### Wallet Connections

Slinq uses **Privy** for secure wallet connections:

- We never see or store your private keys
- Sign-in uses cryptographic signatures
- Session tokens are encrypted
- No password storage on our servers

### Gasless Lazy Launch

Token creation uses a "lazy launch" system with EIP-712 typed signatures:

- You sign a message (called a "Launch Intent"), not a transaction — no gas fees for you
- The token only deploys on-chain when the first buyer arrives
- Replay protection prevents duplicate deploys
- Signature proves ownership without exposing keys
- The first buyer's transaction atomically creates the token, adds liquidity, and executes their buy — all in one step

## Reporting Security Issues

If you discover a vulnerability:

1. **Do not** post publicly
2. Contact us through official channels
3. Provide detailed reproduction steps
4. Allow time for us to fix before disclosure

We take security reports seriously and appreciate responsible disclosure.
