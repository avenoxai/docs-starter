# Launch Your First Token

Choose your preferred method to create a token. Slinq supports Ethereum launches through its Clanker wrapper and BNB Chain launches through PancakeSwap.

<Info>
Social and agent launches default to Ethereum unless you explicitly ask for BNB Chain with wording like "on BSC", "on BNB", or "on PancakeSwap".
</Info>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Tokenize a Post" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    The fastest way to create a token — reply to any viral tweet or cast.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Find a great post">
        Spot a tweet or Farcaster cast that deserves a token.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Reply with a keyword">
        Reply mentioning `@slinqmeme` (Twitter) or `@slinq` (Farcaster):
        ```
        @slinqmeme tokenize this
        ```
      </Step>
      <Step title="AI creates the token">
        Slinq's AI generates a name, ticker, and description from the post. The original author becomes the creator.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Start trading">
        The token is live instantly. Share the link from the bot's reply.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>The original post author earns creator revenue. You can tokenize anyone's post — it's like gifting them a revenue stream.</Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Twitter" icon="x-twitter">
    Launch your own token directly from your Twitter feed.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Compose your tweet">
        Post a tweet mentioning `@slinqmeme` with your token details:
        ```
        @slinqmeme deploy $MOON "Moon Token"
        ```
      </Step>
      <Step title="Wait for confirmation">
        Within seconds, you'll receive a reply with your token's contract address and trading link.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Start trading">
        Click the link to view your token on Slinq. Trading is live immediately.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>Your Twitter handle becomes the token creator. On BNB Chain hook launches, creators can earn up to 40% of the Slinq hook fee.</Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Farcaster" icon="message">
    Launch tokens natively from Warpcast or any Farcaster client.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Cast your token">
        Post a cast mentioning `@slinq` with your token details:
        ```
        @slinq deploy $BASED "Based Token"
        ```
      </Step>
      <Step title="Receive confirmation">
        Slinq replies to your cast with the token address and trading link.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Share and trade">
        Your followers can trade immediately from the embedded link.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Telegram" icon="paper-plane">
    Launch and manage tokens from Telegram.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the Slinq Bot">
        Find `@slinqmemebot` on Telegram and send `/start`.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Connect your wallet">
        Link your wallet through the bot's Web App.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Create your token">
        Follow the prompts to set your token name and symbol.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Launch and share">
        Your token goes live. Share the link in your Telegram groups.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Web Interface" icon="browser">
    Full control over every aspect of your token.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Connect your wallet">
        Visit [slinq.meme](https://slinq.meme) and connect with any supported wallet.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click Launch Token">
        Find the "Launch Token" button in the navigation bar.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Fill in token details">
        Enter your token name, symbol, description, and upload an image.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Set your FDV">
        Choose your launch chain and starting Fully Diluted Valuation. Ethereum uses ETH presets; BNB Chain uses BNB settings.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sign and deploy">
        Review your token, sign the transaction, and launch.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Info>The web interface lets you add social links, custom images, and detailed descriptions.</Info>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## What Happens Next

Once your token is created:

1. **Liquidity is added** — Your token is paired with ETH/WETH on Ethereum or BNB/WBNB on BNB Chain
2. **LP is locked or routed through the launch rail** — BNB Chain liquidity is locked permanently; Ethereum follows the Clanker wrapper flow
3. **Trading goes live** — Anyone can buy and sell your token immediately
4. **You start earning** — Creator revenue accumulates according to the launch rail

## Token Parameters

Every Slinq token has these properties:

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Total Supply | 100 Billion tokens |
| Starting FDV | Depends on launch method |
| Ethereum Default FDV | 2 ETH |
| Ethereum Presets | 1 / 2 / 5 / 10 ETH |
| BNB Chain Default FDV | 10 BNB |
| BNB Chain Web FDV | 50 - 50,000 BNB |
| Trading Pair | TOKEN/WETH on Ethereum, TOKEN/WBNB on BNB Chain |
| Creator Share | Chain-specific creator revenue; up to 40% on BNB Chain hook launches |
