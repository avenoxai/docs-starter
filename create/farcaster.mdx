# Farcaster Token Launch

Create tokens directly from Warpcast or any Farcaster client by casting at `@slinq`.

## Cast Format

```
@slinq deploy $TICKER "Token Name"
```

**Examples:**
```
@slinq deploy $BASED "Based Token"
@slinq create $FCAST "Farcaster Coin"
@slinq launch $DEGEN "Degen Mode"
```

<Tip>
Keywords `deploy`, `create`, `launch`, `make`, and `build` all work.
</Tip>

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Cast your token">
    Post a cast mentioning `@slinq` with your ticker and name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Slinq processes the cast">
    The Farcaster bot validates your request and prepares the deployment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Token goes live">
    Your token launches on Ethereum through the Slinq Clanker wrapper by default, or on BNB Chain when you explicitly ask for BSC/BNB/PancakeSwap.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirmation cast">
    Slinq replies to your cast with:
    - Contract address
    - Trading link
    - Creator dashboard link
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|------------|---------|
| **Farcaster Account** | Active account on any client |
| **Ticker** | 1-10 characters, alphanumeric |
| **Name** | 1-50 characters |
| **Daily Limit** | 4 tokens per Farcaster ID |

## Token Parameters

Farcaster launches use these defaults:

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Default Chain | Ethereum |
| Ethereum Starting FDV | 2 ETH |
| BNB Chain Starting FDV | 10 BNB |
| Total Supply | 100 Billion |
| Trading Pair | TOKEN/WETH on Ethereum, TOKEN/WBNB on BNB Chain |
| Image | Custom upload or AI-generated |
| Description | AI-generated from your cast |

## Farcaster-Specific Features

### Frame Integration

Slinq's confirmation casts include Farcaster Frames, letting your followers:
- See token stats at a glance
- Trade directly from the frame
- Share with one tap

### Social Context

Your Farcaster profile becomes linked to your token:
- Profile picture appears on token page
- Username shown as creator
- Followers can find your tokens easily

## Common Issues

<Accordion title="Cast not detected">
  - Ensure you're mentioning `@slinq` (not `@Slinq` or other variations)
  - Check that your cast is public
  - Wait 1-2 minutes—processing can take a moment
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Ticker unavailable">
  Tickers are unique across the platform. Try:
  - Adding numbers: `$BASED2`
  - Using variations: `$BSED` instead of `$BASED`
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Daily limit reached">
  Farcaster allows 4 tokens per day. Use the [web interface](/create/web) for additional launches or wait until midnight UTC.
</Accordion>

## Tokenize Someone Else's Cast

You can also turn someone else's cast into a token by replying:

```
@slinq tokenize this
```

The **original cast author** becomes the token creator and earns fees. This is called [social tokenization](/create/social-tokenization).

<Tip>
Social tokenization keywords: tokenize, tokenise, make this a token, coin this, launch this, mint this
</Tip>
