# Telegram Token Launch

Use the Slinq Telegram bot to create tokens, track launches, and get real-time notifications — all without leaving Telegram.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the Slinq Bot">
    Search for `@slinqmemebot` on Telegram or join our community at [t.me/slinqxyz](https://t.me/slinqxyz).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a conversation">
    Send `/start` to the bot to begin.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your wallet">
    Link your wallet through the bot's Web App interface.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch or trade">
    Use the bot to create tokens, browse launches, and get alerts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Token Parameters

Tokens created via Telegram use these defaults:

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Starting FDV | 10 WBNB (customizable) |
| Total Supply | 100 Billion |
| Trading Pair | TOKEN/WBNB |
| Image | Custom upload or AI-generated |
| Description | Auto-generated |

<Info>
You can upload a custom image during the launch flow. All launch methods support custom images.
</Info>

## Common Questions

<Accordion title="Is the Telegram bot official?">
  Yes. The official bot is `@slinqmemebot`. Be cautious of impersonators — Slinq will never DM you first or ask for your private keys.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do I need BNB in my wallet?">
  You need BNB for trading and gas fees. Token creation itself is gasless (you just sign a message).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I use Telegram and Twitter together?">
  Absolutely. Your tokens and earnings are linked to your wallet, not the platform. Create on Twitter, manage on Telegram, trade on the web — it all connects.
</Accordion>
