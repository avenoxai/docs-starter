# Supported Chains

Slinq now supports two launch rails: **Ethereum through the Slinq Clanker wrapper** and **BNB Chain through PancakeSwap**. The app handles the routing for you, so creators can launch from social or web without learning the underlying contracts.

## The Short Version

| Chain | Launch rail | Best for |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Ethereum** | Slinq ETH Clanker wrapper | Default social and agent launches, Ethereum-native communities, Clanker ecosystem distribution |
| **BNB Chain** | PancakeSwap Infinity or PancakeSwap V3 | Low-cost BNB trading, Slinq hook fees, PancakeSwap-native launches |

<Info>
If you do not choose a chain, Slinq routes social and agent launches to Ethereum by default. Use explicit wording like "on BSC", "on BNB", or "on PancakeSwap" when you want the BNB Chain path.
</Info>

## Ethereum Launches

Ethereum launches use Slinq's wrapper around Clanker's SDK and contracts. You still launch from Slinq, but the token is created through the proven Clanker flow on Ethereum.

## BNB Chain Launches

BNB Chain launches use Slinq's PancakeSwap stack. Depending on the launch route, tokens can use PancakeSwap Infinity hooks or PancakeSwap V3.

## What Changes For Users?

| Topic | Ethereum | BNB Chain |
|-------|----------|-----------|
| Gas token | ETH | BNB |
| Pair | TOKEN/WETH | TOKEN/WBNB |
| Default starting FDV | 2 ETH | 10 BNB for normal launches |
| Social launch default | Yes | Only when explicitly requested |
| Trading venue | Clanker-compatible Ethereum flow | PancakeSwap Infinity or V3 |
| Creator rewards | Creator-facing Clanker/Slinq revenue shown in ETH | Up to 40% creator share on Slinq hook or V3 harvested fee rewards |
