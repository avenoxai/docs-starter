# Trading on Slinq

Every new Slinq token is tradeable instantly after launch — no bonding curve, no migration. Ethereum tokens trade through Slinq's Clanker wrapper flow, while BNB Chain tokens trade through PancakeSwap Infinity or V3.

## How Trading Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a token">
    Browse the homepage, search by name/ticker, or click a link from social media.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your wallet">
    Click "Connect Wallet" and choose your preferred method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter trade amount">
    Type how much ETH or BNB you want to spend, depending on the token's launch chain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and sign">
    Review the quote and sign the transaction in your wallet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Trading Fees

| Fee Type | Amount | Who Gets It |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| Fee Type | Ethereum | BNB Chain |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| Creator revenue | Clanker/Slinq creator-facing revenue shown in ETH terms | Up to 40% of Slinq hook fees, or V3 harvested fee rewards |
| Launch protection | Clanker wrapper flow | PancakeSwap hook protection on Infinity launches |
| Gas | ETH | BNB |

<Info>
The quote you see includes the relevant route fees for that chain.
</Info>

## What You Need

To trade on Slinq:

1. **A wallet** — MetaMask, WalletConnect, or embedded wallet
2. **ETH or BNB** — For gas fees and buying tokens
3. **The right network** — Ethereum for ETH launches, BNB Chain for BSC launches
