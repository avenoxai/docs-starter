# Trading on Slinq

Every new Slinq token is tradeable instantly after launch — no bonding curve, no migration. Trading happens natively on PancakeSwap Infinity through our integrated interface, powered by a hook that manages fees and launch protection automatically.

## How Trading Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a token">
    Browse the homepage, search by name/ticker, or click a link from social media.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your wallet">
    Click "Connect Wallet" and choose your preferred method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter trade amount">
    Type how much BNB you want to spend (buying) or tokens to sell.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and sign">
    Review the quote and sign the transaction in your wallet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Trading Fees

| Fee Type | Amount | Who Gets It |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| Base Fee | 1% | Split below |
| Creator Share | 0.35-0.4% | Token creator or beneficiary |
| First Buyer Share | 0-0.05% | Person who triggered the on-chain launch |
| Protocol Share | 0.6% | Slinq platform |
| Gas | Variable | BNB network |

<Info>
Fees are managed by a PancakeSwap V4 hook and automatically deducted from your trade. The quote you see includes all fees.
</Info>

## What You Need

To trade on Slinq:

1. **A wallet** — MetaMask, WalletConnect, or embedded wallet
2. **BNB** — For gas fees and buying tokens
3. **Connected to BNB Chain** — Chain ID 56
